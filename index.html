Data Visualizations:


Airline Safety Metrics Explorer

This visualization is created on Tableau by joining two datasets from FiveThirtyEight and NationsOnline. It gives a snapshot of the safety metrics related to different airlines around the world.


Employment Insights: Trends, Majors, and Degrees

Developed a Tableau dashboard to analyze the employment trends across majors and over the years. Created using the dataset provided by the Federal Reserve Bank of New York, it gives an overview of the changing landscape of employment.

Data Analysis & Machine Learning:


Clickbait or Connection? Cracking the Code of Likes on YouTube's Trending Page

As part of the Unsupervised ML coursework, led a team of 4 people to perform sentiment analysis of YouTube videos to understand user preferences and help improve the platform.


Data Driven Lending: A Study in Risk Management

Delved into risk management by building a supervised ML project to help predict if a loan will default or not, based on the key performing indicators thereby helping mitigate the risks associated with loan defaulters.

BigData Project:

Using Google Cloud and BigQuery, my team and I analyzed the impact of COVID-19 on NYC taxi ridership. The analysis encompasses economic shifts, payment trends, fluctuations in trip patterns, and the resilience of key transportation hubs, offering a comprehensive view of the industry's response to dynamic market forces and empowering stakeholders to navigate future challenges effectively.
